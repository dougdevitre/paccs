Each subdirectory contains copy/paste-ready materials organized by audience and channel. All content aligns with the **PACCS Five Pillars of Child-Centered Safety** and has been developed using evidence-based, neutral, professional language suitable for public distribution.

## PACCS Five Pillars of Child-Centered Safety

All outreach materials are grounded in the Five Pillars:

1. **Emotional Safety** — Protecting stable, secure attachment
2. **Psychological Health** — Preventing fear, rejection, role-reversal
3. **Truth & Evidence** — Grounding decisions in validated research
4. **Protection from Abuse of Systems** — Recognizing false allegations and litigation abuse as harm
5. **Family Preservation & Healing** — Supporting ethical, evidence-based interventions
