## How to Use These Campaigns

Each campaign includes:
- **Campaign overview** and target audience profile
- **Key messages** tailored to the audience
- **4-phase rollout** (Awareness → Engagement → Activation → Sustained Action)
- **4-week content calendar** with specific activities per channel
- **10-15 ready-to-use social media posts** with hashtags
- **3-email welcome sequence** (introduction, follow-up, call to action)
- **Downloadable resources** linked to PACCS template/script/outreach library
- **Success metrics** and KPIs

### Running a Campaign

1. Select the campaign that matches your target audience
2. Customize messages and posts for your local context (state, community, events)
3. Follow the phased rollout timeline
4. Use the content calendar as a starting point — adjust frequency to your capacity
5. Track metrics and adjust based on what resonates
6. Cross-reference with `processes/outreach-campaign-planning.md` for detailed workflow

## PACCS Five Pillars (All Campaigns Align With)

1. **Emotional Safety** — Protecting stable, secure attachment
2. **Psychological Health** — Preventing fear, rejection, role-reversal
3. **Truth & Evidence** — Grounding decisions in validated research
4. **Protection from System Abuse** — Recognizing false allegations and litigation abuse as harm
5. **Family Preservation & Healing** — Supporting ethical, evidence-based interventions
