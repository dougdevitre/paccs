## Production Notes

- **Tone:** Careful, balanced, evidence-based. This is a sensitive topic — avoid any implication that allegations should not be investigated.
- **Narrator voice:** Professional, measured, neutral.
- **Music:** Subtle, serious but not dramatic.
- **Key framing:** Always pair "false allegations are a problem" with "all allegations must be investigated." Never suggest that children should not be believed — emphasize proper investigation.
- **Accessibility:** Closed captions required. High-contrast text. Audio description for visuals.

## Key Research References

- Faller, K.C. (2007). *Interviewing Children about Sexual Abuse.*
