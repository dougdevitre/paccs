# PACCS Process Documents

> Step-by-step operational processes for PACCS committees, volunteers, and advocates engaged in outreach, education, and legislative engagement.

---

## How to Use These Processes

- **Follow each process sequentially** from planning through evaluation.
- **Adapt templates** to your specific campaign, audience, or jurisdiction.
- **Track progress** using the checklists and tracking formats provided in each document.
- **Coordinate with PACCS committees** before launching external-facing campaigns or legislative outreach.
- **Document results** so future efforts can build on lessons learned.

## Process Development Guidelines

All PACCS processes should:

1. **Be evidence-based** — grounded in research and best practices
2. **Be actionable** — provide concrete steps, not just theory
3. **Include templates** — give users tools they can immediately put to work
4. **Include metrics** — define how success will be measured
5. **Be reviewed** — undergo committee review before publication
6. **Be updated** — reviewed at least annually for accuracy and relevance
