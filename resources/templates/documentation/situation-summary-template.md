# One-Page Situation Summary Template

> **Audience:** Parents, Attorneys, GALs, Therapists, Evaluators
> **Purpose:** Provide a concise, one-page overview of your situation that any professional can review in under five minutes.
> **Related Skill:** paccs-tech-advocate

---

## How to Use This Template

- Fill in every section with brief, factual information. This is a summary, not a full narrative.
- Use bullet points, not paragraphs. Professionals are busy and need to scan quickly.
- Update this document before every meeting with a new professional (attorney consultation, GAL interview, therapist intake).
- Redact real names and use initials or pseudonyms in any digital drafts.
- Bring a printed copy to in-person meetings. Offer it at the beginning of the conversation.

---

## Tips

- Keep this to one page when printed. If you need more space, attach supporting documents separately.
- Update the "Prepared" date every time you revise it so professionals know they are reading the current version.
- Practice summarizing your situation verbally in two minutes or less, using this document as your guide.
- Avoid emotional language. "Court-ordered visitation was denied on three consecutive dates" is more effective than "My ex keeps stealing my time."
- Bring extra copies. You may meet with multiple people in a single visit.
