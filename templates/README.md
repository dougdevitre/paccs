# PACCS Templates

> A collection of ready-to-use templates for parents and families navigating parental alienation, contact denial, false allegations, and coercive control.

---

## How to Use These Templates

- **Download or copy/paste** any template into your own document, journal, or binder.
- **Customize** the bracketed fields `[like this]` with your own information.
- **Redact** all real names and identifying details before sharing drafts digitally.
- **Store securely** in multiple locations (cloud backup, physical binder, attorney's office).
- **Review with a professional** before submitting anything to court or sharing with institutions.

## Important Notes

- All templates use `YYYY-MM-DD` date format for consistency and court readiness.
- Replace all `[bracketed text]` with your own information before use.
- Never include real names in digital drafts. Use initials or pseudonyms.
- These templates are starting points. Your attorney or therapist may recommend modifications.
- Document positive interactions, not just negative ones. Courts notice balanced records.
